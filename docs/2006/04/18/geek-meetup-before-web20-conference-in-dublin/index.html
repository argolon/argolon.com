Link: Attention lowly bloggers: Sip from the cup of Canter wisdom

Robert Smith has arranged a meet-up in the Market Bar off Sth Great Georges Street for next Wednesday April 26th. This is a Gastropub-style place which specialises in Tapas. To avoid meet-up analysis-paralysis Robert has smartly just specified the location and post-6pm as the time. I’ll be driving up from Cork so I’ll be there a little later.

I’m looking forward to meeting a good gaggle of Irish Bloggers, those working in the tech industry here, Marc Canter and hopefully some of the other speakers.

In case you don’t know Dublin, Fade Street is here.
