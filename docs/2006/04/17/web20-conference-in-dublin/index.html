On Thursday week, there is a Web2.0 conference in Dublin. There are many reasons for attending but two speakers make it a must see:

  • Marc Canter of Broadband Mechanics is a legend in the tech world. His will not be a boring speech! This blog posting would not have been done in hCalendar format using the Structured Blogging plug-in if it was not for his infectious evangelism.

  • Walter Higgins of Sxoop, creator of PXN8.com is a poster boy for how to avoid hot air and generate revenue from a product on day one. Walter is a great guy with a razor sharp mind.

Other speakers (from the Web2Ireland site) include: Judy Gibbons, Venture Partner, Accel; Jeff Clavier, SoftTechVC; Daniel Waterhouse, 3i; Nasser Batley, DRKW; Adam Green, Dawinian Web
