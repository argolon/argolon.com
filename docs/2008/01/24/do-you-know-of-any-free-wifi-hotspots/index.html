Do you know of any free Wifi Hotspots?

James Corbett is building a map of Wifi Hotspots around the country. Whilst they can be free or non-free, I hope we can identify as many free ones as possible first. I’ve added the ones I know of in Cork and indicated whether they are publicly accessible locations or not.

If you know of any, please spread the love and add a push-pin to the map. You should also include any FON hotspots that you know of.

UPDATE 1: Don’t forget Eircom’s map of their hotspots. These currently cost me nothing as an Eircom broadband subscriber. Hopefully they will extend the scheme indefinitely.

UPDATE 2: Also don’t forget the map of FON Hotspots. These are free for Linus FONeros and cheap for others. There aren’t many in Ireland but hopefully we’ll grow it more.
